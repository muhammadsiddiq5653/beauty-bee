Platform Deep Dive

How to Win on Each Platform

Specific tactics, post frequency, and what content works best per platform.

🎵

TikTok Top Priority

Post: 1–2x daily · Best time: 7–9pm PKT · Format: 15–60 sec vertical video
  • Hook in first 1.5 seconds — show the transformation immediately
  • Use trending sounds from TikTok's Commercial Sound Library
  • Hashtags: #LipTint #PakistaniBeauty #GlowUp #BeautyTips #MakeupPK
  • Caption: one question to drive comments ("which shade are you?")
  • Reply to every comment in first hour — TikTok rewards engagement
  • Stitch/Duet popular beauty videos to borrow their audience
  • Link: website in bio via one.link or Linktree
📘

Facebook Medium

Post: 4–5x/week · Use Meta Business Suite to auto-cross-post from Instagram
  • Create Facebook Page — mirrors your Instagram content automatically
  • Create private Facebook Group: "Beauty Bee Girls Pakistan" — community
  • Post polls, questions, shade votes in the group weekly
  • Facebook Reels performing very well — upload natively for more reach
  • Run a Facebook giveaway monthly — "share + tag a friend to win"
  • Facebook Shop — sync your products for in-app discovery
📌

Pinterest Medium

Post: 5–10 pins/day (can schedule) · Traffic peaks 3–6 months after posting
  • Claim your website in Pinterest settings (boosts trust + SEO)
  • Create boards: "Pakistani Makeup Looks", "Lip Tint Shades", "Skincare Routine"
  • Pin every product photo, lifestyle shot, tutorial screenshot
  • Write keyword-rich descriptions: "organic lip tint Pakistan cash on delivery"
  • Create Idea Pins (multi-frame tutorials) — highest reach format
  • Use Tailwind app to schedule 30 days of pins in one sitting
▶️

YouTube Shorts Medium

Post: 3–4x/week Shorts + 1 long video/month · Feeds Google search
  • Shorts: repurpose your TikToks (same content, upload natively)
  • Long video ideas: "I tried 6 lip tints for 30 days", "Full glam tutorial"
  • Title formula: "Pakistan's Best Lip Tint? We Tested All 6 Shades"
  • Add your website link in every video description
  • YouTube description = Google-indexed — use keywords naturally
  • Community tab: polls, shade vote, behind the scenes
📧

Email Marketing Top Priority

Send: 1x/week · Tool: Mailchimp (free up to 500) or Klaviyo · Already collecting!
  • Welcome email: sent immediately with 10% discount code
  • Week 2: "How to apply lip & cheek tint" tutorial
  • Week 3: Customer story / before & after
  • Week 4: New shade spotlight or bundle deal
  • Segment: buyers vs non-buyers — different messages
  • Abandoned cart email: "You forgot your shade!" — highest ROI email
🔍

Google SEO + GBP Long Game

Takes 3–6 months · Free · Highest intent traffic
  • Claim Google Business Profile at business.google.com (free)
  • Add all products, photos, hours, WhatsApp number
  • Ask every customer to leave a Google review
  • Write 2 blog posts/month: "Best lip tint for Pakistani skin tones"
  • Target keywords: "lip tint Pakistan", "organic lip colour COD"
  • Add alt text to all product images with keywords

Content Ideas by Format

Never run out of ideas. Mix these content types to keep your audience engaged and attract new followers.

💡 The Repurposing Rule

Create once → publish everywhere. One 30-second video = TikTok + Instagram Reel + YouTube Short + Facebook Reel + Pinterest Video Pin. This saves 80% of your content creation time.

📹 Short Video (15–60 sec)

Transformation Content

Before & After Application Bare lips → full colour in 10 seconds. Most viral format in beauty.
GRWM (Get Ready With Me) Morning routine featuring your lip tint. Relatable, high retention.
All 6 Shades in 60 Seconds Swatch all shades on lips — shade comparison videos always go viral.
Morning → Night Transformation Same tint, different looks. Shows versatility of the product.

Technical Setup

How to Get Your Pixel IDs

The code is already in your website. You just need to fill in 3 values in your .env.local file.

✅ Already Done For You

GA4, Meta Pixel, and TikTok Pixel scripts are already embedded in your website layout. Just paste your IDs into .env.local and redeploy.

Step 1

🔍 Google Analytics 4

  1. Go to analytics.google.com
  2. Create account → Add property
  3. Choose Web → Enter your website URL
  4. Copy your Measurement ID (G-XXXXXXX)
  5. Paste into .env.local as NEXT_PUBLIC_GA_MEASUREMENT_ID
Step 2

📘 Meta (Facebook) Pixel

  1. Go to business.facebook.com
  2. Events Manager → Connect Data Source
  3. Choose Web → Name your pixel
  4. Copy your Pixel ID (16-digit number)
  5. Paste into .env.local as NEXT_PUBLIC_FB_PIXEL_ID
Step 3

🎵 TikTok Pixel

  1. Go to ads.tiktok.com
  2. Assets → Events → Web Events
  3. Manage → Set Up Web Events → Manual
  4. Copy your Pixel ID
  5. Paste into .env.local as NEXT_PUBLIC_TIKTOK_PIXEL_ID

YOUR .env.local — replace X values with real IDs

NEXT_PUBLIC_GA_MEASUREMENT_ID=G-XXXXXXXXXX
NEXT_PUBLIC_FB_PIXEL_ID=XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX
NEXT_PUBLIC_TIKTOK_PIXEL_ID=XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX

After editing .env.local → run npm run build or redeploy to Vercel.
